Elijah liremi

My name is Elijah liremi,I was born in makhitha village on the 5 November 1989 about 30 km western side of Louis trichardt. I am the Last born and only a boy of mother makwarela matia and father mafhungo William liremi, my father from the royal house belonging to the clan of king mapungubwe known as Tshivhula of vhatwanamba nation commonly known as vhadau. Born in the midst of poverty and underdevelopment I did well at primary school (madaheni combined school) and secondary school a(madaheni combined school) and then graduated to university level. Of cause a lot of expectations from my mother who raised us 06 children under difficult conditions since her husband died in 1998 when I was doing grade 3 but through the mentorship of my former teacher Mrs mudau known as (alody)I manage to survive and let it go. My life has been of struggle and victory, a bigger challenge to face when one had to study and think of poverty which doesn't only affect you alone , but with the inspiration of my brother takalani Telekisa who manange to exell Without th Availabity of his mother I manange to understand that this too shall pass I remember when my sisters force me to study social worker cause of the poverty we had faced at home I manange to settle for political science cause of the ispriration of Dr phandelani mathoma who was by dat time an ambassador of Mali in south Africa embassy, At university I became part of the progressive youth alliance which i had to fight against students financial exclusion in high education. I grew up within the ranks to become a 1prominent leader of sasco, ancyl,ycl and anc and given my family , community background of poverty of cause a staunch communist in fact I joined anc at the age of 15 before I become a member of the Anc under the influence of the late Elias Rantete and by that time he was running to be a ward councilor but due to betrayal he lost to johana nemalengeni, With those young people of my generation cedrick Mokoena, jack Lithole and comrade Jacob khoza who became my friend after The formation of Liremi career foundation, we became found of classics we could read karl marx from the beginners, origin of money and family, thesis on pheubac to lenin, stalin, trosky and rousa luxenberg. Ours was to critique conditions we lived in at time our background and analyse the envisaged noble future of society we would love to see. Under the guidance of great young adults revolutionaries cde vuyani Sokhaba who was 2014/2015 president of src at university of the western cape, Jackson mirahe ,norman zwannda Kutama and late elias Rantete who was direct mentor and blood brother we became part of broader mass democratic movement. At university of the western cape I became close friend with Nwaoko prof Mashao but rather blood brother an academic of Univen rather now a nuclear physics.Having been inspired by leaders in the anc such as president mbeki, Joel peter mayibuye netshitendzhe, ngwako ramatlodi and sello moloto who in my cosas was inspired to meet them, I come from the generation which led us in our early youth the generation of Jackson mirahe, Vuyo Yekani, Tshifhiwa matakala,the late thembi mokgonyana and others. I was a prominent member of Ycl in uwc,sasco in my university days of cape Town and of cause district committee member of the young communist league of south Africa Alpheuos malivha district far north of limppopo. My personal friend I grew up with in my home village phathutshedzo ndouvhada died at university in his last year of jounalism in the university of limpopo, losing a young man who was a close friend to my heart was one thing bad, cause a day before his death he said to me now that your political science student the future of the youth belong to you especially makhitha village if you further need help tell me, after his burial in 2011 I establish liremi career foundation to help the children of the poor and working class, Long live the struggle of our people against hunger and underdevelopment for economic emancipation. We are indid highlighting south African possibilities.

Mudau Thabelo Rodney was born in 1989 at Ha-Mashau Village. He is the last born of Mr and Mrs Mudau. He has interest in sports, live events, business adventures and youth development and empowerment. Thabelo is highly influenced by people who comes from humble begginings that strives for sucess and community development at large.

Thabelo attended primary and secondary schools at Ha-Mashau village and matriculated in 2005. He attained his BSC (Diet) Hons degree at Medunsa in 2009 and completed his masters degree in the University of Limpopo, Medunsa campus in 2014. He intended to further obtain more degrees in different fields of study, with keen interest in business administration. Thabelo has been working for the department of Heath for the past 6 years and currently works in Louis Trichardt memorial hospital as a Health professional, He is the finance officer of Liremi Career Foundation.
