Mpho Nembudani

Operational Manager

Mpho Nembudani born in Shayandima Limpopo but raised in Louis Trichardt Limpopo is the former President of the Makhado Center for Youth Advice (2003-2006) and former Project Manager of Miss Makhado Beauty Pageant (2003-2006

She completed her civil engineering studies in 2009 at the Tshwane South College, she then held a position as an apprentice for the City Of Tshwane Municipality years 2010-2012 where she obtained her trade certificate in plumbing(2012). Amongst her other achievements she chaired leadership positions in tertiary and churches (head of departments and events organizing committee). She joined the small business enterprise industry in 2004 when she registered her company World Focus 376 providing trade and supply Services as well as transport services for different trips and tours.she registered her second company Vhakoni Events Co-ordinators in 2006 which was more focused on providing catering,entertainment and promotional services

She is currently the CED of Twinsticle Modelling and Promotions, sole Director of Eventful Lifestyle and chairs on the managing committee of Pathfinders Community Developers.she is an executive events director for TJ Malamule Foundation and a Director at The Elegant Ladies Connection.She is also studying further part time pursuing a Business Management-Marketing qualification With all opportunities before her she strives to always be the best Personal Development Coach, Civil Engineer, Plumber, Student, Business Woman and Multi-Millionaire in the making.The words of the legendary Michael Jackson: “I'm interested in making a path instead of following a trail” keep her moving forward and focused on her goals and lives to prove those words to be a reality.

With all the opportunities that lie before me I strives to always be the best. Personal Development Coach, Civil Engineer, Plumber, Student, Business Woman and Multi-Millionaire in the making I go by the words of the legendary Michael Jackson: “I’m interested in making a path instead of following a trail” I lived and proven this to be true.

Elijah Liremi

Managing Director

. Elijah liremi was born in Makhitha village, about 30 km western side of Louis Trichardt.Being the last born and only son born in the midst of poverty in an underdeveloped village,he was pushed by circumstances to do well at my primary and secondary school levels at Madaheni Combined School until he graduated to university level. So many expectations from his family and unfortunate background put pressure on him ,but the mentorship from his former teacher Mrs Mudau who was also known as Alody , he managed to survive the hardship and survive the scars of his background.His life has been struggle that lead to victory. One person who played a dominant role in inspiring him was his brother Takalani Telekisa who in spite of having lost his mother he excelled.choosing his career path was not as easy since he had his sisters pushing him to settle for a different career,but in the end he chose political sciences.this was because of inspiration from Dr Phandelani Mathoma who was at that time an ambassador of mali at the South African Embassy. At university he became part of the progressive youth alliance(PYA)where he fought against student financial exclusion.he still strives to achieve more.

Takalani Telekisa

Communication Manager

Takalani was born and breed in Limpopo province formerly known as theRepublic of Venda, a very active and passionate man who started formal schooling at Mmbabada Primary School in 1990 and also attended Muduluni senior primary school. Ramasela Carol Ledwaba

Marketing Manager

After matriculating, she enrolled with the University of Johannesburg to pursuer a qualification in human resource management as she had so much compassion During her varsity years, she was elected for the position Resident Academic Advisor for the residence hall called Hector Peterson in the year 2012 at the University of Johannesburg.
