Mpho Nembudani

Operational Manager

My name is Mpho Nembudani i am from Shayandima but raised in Louis Trichardt Limpopo. I am a former President of Makhado Center for Youth Advice (2003-2006) and Project Manager of Miss Makhado Beauty Pageant (2003-2006).

Amongst other achievements I have chaired leadership positions in tertiary and churches (head of departments and events organising committee). I started in the small business enterprise industry in 2004 when I registered my company World Focus 376 which was based on trading and supply as well as organising transportation for trips and tours. In 2006 I registered my second company Vhakoni Events Co-ordinators and was dealing with catering, entertainment and promotions.

Completed my civil engineering studies in 2009 at Tshwane South College, was an apprentice at the City of Tshwane Municipality from 2010-2012 and obtained my plumbing trade certificate in 2012. I am currently the CED of Twinsticle Modelling and Promotions, sole Director of Eventful Lifestyle and chairs on the managing committee of Pathfinders Community Developers as well as studying part time for a Business Management-Marketing qualification. I am also the executive events director for the TJ Malamule Foundation and a Director at The Elegant Ladies Connection.

With all the opportunities that lie before me I strives to always be the best. Personal Development Coach, Civil Engineer, Plumber, Student, Business Woman and Multi-Millionaire in the making I go by the words of the legendary Michael Jackson: “I’m interested in making a path instead of following a trail” I lived and proven this to be true.

Elijah Liremi

Managing Director

My name is Elijah Liremi I was born in Makhitha village on the 5th November 1989 about 30 km Western side of Louis Trichardt. I am the Last born and only a boy of mother Makwarela Matia and father Mafhungo William Liremi.

I was born in the midst of poverty and underdevelopment. I did well at primary school (Madaheni combined school) and secondary school (Madaheni combined school) and then graduated to university level. Of cause a lot of expectations from my mother who raised us 06 children under difficult conditions since her husband died in 1998 when I was doing grade 3 but through the mentorship of my former teacher Mrs mudau known as (Alody)I manage to survive and let it go. My life has been of struggle and victory, a bigger challenge to face when one had to study and think of poverty which doesn't only affect you alone , but with the inspiration of my brother takalani Telekisa who manange to exell Without th Availabity of his mother I manange to understand that this too shall pass I remember when my sisters force me to study social worker cause of the poverty we had faced at home I manange to settle for political science cause of the ispriration of Dr phandelani mathoma who was by dat time an ambassador of Mali in south Africa embassy, At university I became part of the progressive youth alliance which I had to fight against students financial exclusion in high education.

Thabelo Mudau

Finance Officer

Mudau Thabelo Rodney was born in 1989 at Ha-Mashau Village. He is the last born of Mr and Mrs Mudau. He has interest in sports, live events, business adventures and youth development and empowerment. Thabelo is highly influenced by people who comes from humble begginings that strives for sucess and community development at large.

Thabelo attended primary and secondary schools at Ha-Mashau village and matriculated in 2005. He attained his BSC (Diet) Hons degree at Medunsa in 2009 and completed his masters degree in the University of Limpopo, Medunsa campus in 2014. He intended to further obtain more degrees in different fields of study, with keen interest in business administration. Thabelo has been working for the department of Heath for the past 6 years and currently works in Louis Trichardt memorial hospital as a Health professional, He is the finance officer of Liremi Career Foundation.

Ramasela Carol Ledwaba

Marketing Manager

Ramasela Carol Ledwaba was born in 1990 at Mosesetjane Village, Limpopo.

Carol has dreams and ambitions of adding significant value to the HR industry, she sees herself being integral parts in people’s lives by helping them further their education and also assist them getting and making a career out of their dreams. After matriculating, carol enrolled with the University of Johannesburg in order to feed the love she has for human resource management by studying towards a Diploma in human resource management.

She believes in education being the ultimate key success, with that she is determined to further her studies by studying towards a BTech degree and then later a Bcom honours in human resource management.

During her varsity years, she was elected to be Resident Academic Advisor for the resident called Hector Peterson in the year 2012 at the University of Johannesburg. Her duties involved assisting first year’s students with adapting to the university diversity and with academics.

Carol is currently participating on an 18months internship at EOH recruitment solutions where she will potentially grow to become a recruitment consultant.

Tshifhiwa Delicia Mudau
